An Interface Between Critics and Curators

Two user sets seek clean line of communication

By FRANCIS CACCAVALE

An interface between critics or bloggers, and, museum or gallery curators. Critics will receive content for their site while curators will receive press for their exhibit, that is, a win-win scenario.

This interface will set a clean line of communication between two party sets, streamlining creation of content as to a blog or article. With this line of communication, critics can prepare their research and an ultimate goal of asking a curator for a private walkthrough, OR utilizing Google virtually.
